## The Passage of Time and Our Fleeting Existence

> What passes on is time, and what get spent is us. So before you get totally spent, live that dream you are counting on all your life.

As we journey through life, time relentlessly marches on, and our existence becomes a fleeting moment in the grand scheme of things. This realization serves as a poignant reminder to seize the day and pursue the dreams that have been tucked away in the depths of our hearts for far too long.

## The Cycle of Life and the Buried Dreams

> You grow up, see other kids passing their board exams, getting into the college and then graduate, so do you! You are lucky if you get a job among thousand candidates for a single vacancy otherwise you go for something which can at least earn you daily bread and butter, you get a girl according to your stability and financial conditions, you marry, manufacture children, apply same cycle to them and then on one fine day your heartbeat stops.

The cycle of life often follows a well-trodden path – we grow up, pursue education, secure employment, start a family, and repeat the cycle with our children. However, amidst this routine, our dreams – those cherished aspirations and ambitions – can become buried and forgotten, overshadowed by the demands of daily existence.

## The Call to Reignite Your Dreams

> Don’t push me hard;  
> I will fall off cliff.  
> The stones at the base  
> Will rip my skip apart,  
> And the vultures will wait  
> To see me die.  
> And I will never reach  
> The other end that is far away.  
> I beg you...  
>   
> Don’t worry, O darling.  
> Then came a voice,  
> Drifting through the skies,  
> Finding its way to my soul.  
> "I have got your back,"  
> And of everyone  
> Who dare  
> Dare to dream.  
> Just think, O darling,  
> "What if you could fly?"


The poetic verses serve as a rallying cry, urging us to reignite the flames of our dreams, no matter how daunting the path may seem. For those who dare to dream, a guiding voice reassures us that we have the strength to soar, to break free from the constraints that have held us back.

## Embracing the Journey and Finding Your Calling

Dreams are not static; they evolve and change as we grow and experience life. The key is to understand the underlying connection – the thread that weaves through all your dreams, revealing your true calling. Embrace this journey of self-discovery, and have the courage to pursue a path that aligns with your deepest aspirations, even if it means defying societal norms or challenging conventional wisdom.

## Conclusion: Never Give Up on Your Dreams

> The point being, never ever, ever give up on your dreams. One way or the other 😀

In the end, the message is clear: never, ever give up on your dreams. Whether it's through music, art, science, or any other pursuit, find a way to bring your dreams to life. Forge your own extraordinary path, and let your passion guide you towards a life of fulfillment and purpose. For those who dare to dream, the possibilities are endless, and the sky is truly the limit.
